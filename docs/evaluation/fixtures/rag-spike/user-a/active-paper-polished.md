# Delta-Graph-RAG Polished Manuscript

This active polished version is the trusted manuscript for final wording and current results.

Delta-Graph-RAG combines dense retrieval with a learned bridge-edge classifier over citation graph neighborhoods. The current method does not rely on manual bridge edges.

On the Yanban scholar note benchmark, the polished version reports Recall@5 0.78, MRR 0.64, and citation coverage 91%. The paper states that claims without retrieved support should be marked as insufficient evidence rather than strengthened during polishing.
